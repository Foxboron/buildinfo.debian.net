Quick-start


Submit a .buildinfo:

$ curl-X PUT --data-binary @- --max-time 30 {{ settings.SITE_URL }}{% url "api:submit" %} < my.buildinfo


Upload a GPG-signed .buildinfo:

$ gpg --output=- --clearsign my.buildinfo | curl -X PUT --max-time 30 --data-binary @- {{ settings.SITE_URL }}{% url "api:submit" %}


Upload a GPG-signed .buildinfo with optional ?node=X metadata:

$ gpg --output=- --clearsign my.buildinfo | curl -X PUT --max-time 30 --data-binary @- {{ settings.SITE_URL }}{% url "api:submit" %}?node=$(hostname -a)
